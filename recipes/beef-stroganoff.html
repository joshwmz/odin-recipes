Beef Stroganoff

Description

Golden seared juicy beef strips smothered in sour cream mushroom gravy, an easy 30 minute recipe.

Ingredients

  • 600g scotch fillet steak
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 300g mushrooms
  • 40g butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Flatten steak - Use your fist (or rolling pin or mallet) to flatten the steaks to about 3~4cm thick. Slice into 5mm strips (cut long ones in half), discarding excess fat. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Sear beef - Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet over high heat. Scatter half the beef in the skillet. QUICKLY spread it with tongs. Leave untouched for 30 seconds until browned. Turn beef quickly. Leave untouched for 30 seconds to brown. Immediately remove onto a plate. Don't worry about pink bits and that it will be raw inside. Add remaining 1 tbsp oil and repeat with remaining beef.
  3. Butter, onions, mushrooms - Turn heat down to medium high. Add butter, melt. Then add onions, cook for 1 minute, then add mushrooms. Cook mushrooms until golden. Scrape bottom of fry pan to get all the golden bits off.
  4. Flour and broth - Add flour, cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add half the broth while stirring. Once incorporated, add remaining broth.
  5. Sour cream and mustard - Stir, then add sour cream and mustard. Stir until incorporated (don't worry if it looks split, sour cream will "melt" as it heats).
  6. Creamy - Bring to simmer, reduce heat to medium low. Once thickens to consistency of pouring cream (3 - 5 minutes), adjust salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Finishing touches - Add beef back in (including plate juices). Simmer for 1 minute, then remove from stove immediately.
  8. Serve - Serve over pasta or egg noodles, sprinkle with chives if desired.
